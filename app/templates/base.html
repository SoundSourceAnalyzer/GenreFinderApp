Genre Finder is a top-notch neural network

Classify any sound file's genre with stunning accuracy!


Just upload your file in .au or .mp3 format and our algorithm will analyze the track and label it with one out of ten genres: blues, classical, country, disco, hiphop, jazz, metal, pop, reggae or rock.

In our project we use TensorFlow - an open source library for machine learning. If you want to find out more about our work, please read our report from link below.
